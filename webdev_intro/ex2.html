The Shard

by Gerred Dillon on Friday, April 17, 2015.

The tallest building in Western Europe, the Shard rises 1,016 feet above sea level. It has 72 floors, plus a public observation deck.

Some facts about the Shard:

  • Construction Started in 2009
  • Tallest Building in Western Europe
  • Has 87 floors

Things to do at the Shard:

  1. Stay at the hotel
  2. Eat fish and chips
  3. See London from the Observation Deck
